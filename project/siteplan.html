Site Name: Machine Learning SV

This name is inspired in the purpose of the website (Machine Learning). SV is short for El Salvador.

  • Home
  • Resources
  • My Journey

Site Purpose:

A website to help people stay motivated in their journey of learning ML.

It will contain general information, pathways and goals and also a form or tracker of what the user studied to keep them motivated.

Scenarios:

What is the best path for a beginner aiming to learn ML?

What are some algorithms or resources I can use to start learning?

Color scheme:

According to my research a good color scheme for this project could include the following:

Blue, Black, Light Gray, White and Neon Green for a touch of futuristic and technological side.

Typography:

Inter or Sora for body text and buttons.

Orbitron or Space Grotesk for titles (Futuristic look).
